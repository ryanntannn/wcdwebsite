Frequently Asked Questions

If my bicycle(bought from FIVE7 store) is faulty, what do i do?
Bicycles purchased from FIVE7 Bicycle Company are subject to a 1 month manufactuerer return policy. If your bike has a defect not caused by you, you can come down to the store and get the faulty part replaced. If you are not located in Singapore, you can contact our staff via email for instructions.
Can I send my bicycle here for servicing even if I did not purchase my bicycle from the FIVE7 store?
Of course! But do note that there will be a S$50 maintanence fee
