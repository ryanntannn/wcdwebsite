Not Just a Bike Store.

The Five Seven Bicycle store is not just your average bicycle store as we are here to bring the best bicycle experience to the community. To achieve this, our store offers these services and events to help keep the cycling community strong, keep your bikes fresh and ulimately give our customers the best riding experience

Building and Maintainence

Every bicycle in our store is carefully crafted by our experienced bicycle mechanics, to ensure long lasting and consistant bicycles. Every bicycle purchased from our store is eligible to recieve free maintainence for 3 years, and a discounted maintainence fee after three years. Customers are encouraged to send in their bikes for maintainence every half a year.

Global Delivery

Our store does not only build bikes for local customers as we ship globaly for free for purchases over S$100. We use NinjaGlobal as our shipping partner as they have special delivery options for bicycles to ensure that all the components are properly handled, sent to your local bike store and assembled there.

Community Events

We believe that the a bike is best ridden when ridden in a group, this is why we organise mass rides every friday night for the cycling community to get together and have a fun ride as a family. We also organise and sponsor races such as the annual trickster festival and laneway fixed gear crit.
